French Fries

Who doesn't like french fries?!

Description

The best meal ever!

Ingredients

  • Potatoes
  • Cooking oil (for frying)
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Peel the potatoes.
  2. Cut them into thin strips.
  3. Rinse the strips in cold water.
  4. Dry them well with a towel.
  5. Heat oil in a deep pan.
  6. Fry the potatoes until golden and crispy.
  7. Remove and place them on paper towels.
  8. Sprinkle with salt. Done.
