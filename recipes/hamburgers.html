Hamburgers

Who doesn't like hamburgers?!

Description

A classic sandwich with a grilled beef patty, cheese, veggies, and condiments in a bun.

Ingredients

  • Hamburger bun
  • Ground beef patty
  • Cheese slice
  • Lettuce leaves
  • Tomato slices
  • Pickle slices
  • Ketchup
  • Mustard

Recipe

  1. Cut the bun in half.
  2. Grill the beef patty.
  3. Add cheese on top and let it melt.
  4. Place lettuce on the bottom bun.
  5. Add the patty with melted cheese.
  6. Add sliced tomatoes and pickles.
  7. Squeeze ketchup and mustard.
  8. Place the top bun and serve.
